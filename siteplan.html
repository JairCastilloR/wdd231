CONIGE - Project Site Plan

The partner every contractor needs

Project Description

CONIGE partners with engineers, architects, and contractors to secure new projects and clients. It also facilitates capital financing while using technology to optimize administrative and project management processes.

Purpose

This website informs contractors about CONIGE's partnership model and helps attract qualified associates.

Audience

  • Independent engineers
  • Architects
  • Small and mid-size contractors

Scenarios

  • Scenario 1: A small independent contractor wants to secure more projects but lacks the time and resources to manage client acquisition. They visit the CONIGE website to understand how the partnership works and what benefits they would receive as an associate.
  • Scenario 2: A civil engineer is exploring ways to grow their business and access larger construction opportunities. They browse the CONIGE site to learn the requirements to become an associate and whether they qualify to apply.
